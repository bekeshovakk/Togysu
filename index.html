Infrastructure for people with disabilities

This website is designed to help people learn about accessible infrastructure for people with disabilities in the city. It provides information about places that are convenient and safe to visit, such as buildings with ramps and accessible entrances. The site's goal is to make the city more inclusive and easy to navigate for everyone... With the help of Toǵysu, users can not only find available locations but also actively participate in the life of the city. You can submit reports on unavailable units, add photos, and help us update the map in real time. It is an interactive tool that makes infrastructure issues visible to society and authorities, accelerating the path to a truly smart and inclusive city.

About us

«Toǵysu» is a social initiative aimed at turning Uralsk into a truly inclusive city. In our community, many people with disabilities face difficulties on a daily basis due to the lack of accessible infrastructure, which limits their freedom and social inclusion. Our goal is to bridge this gap by creating a comprehensive digital platform that displays accessible routes and highlights barrier-free spaces. We believe that urban mobility is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Combining modern web technologies with social activity, our project aims to empower every citizen to move around the city with dignity and independence, making the concept of a "smart city" a reality for everyone.

Terms of use

We care about safety. Please try not to capture the faces of passers-by and car license plates when taking pictures to protect personal data.
Each application is checked. Specify the exact name of the place and its correct geolocation (address), otherwise we will not be able to publish the place.
The photo should clearly show the problem (steep ramp, lack of exit). The clearer the photo, the faster we will draw attention to the site.
